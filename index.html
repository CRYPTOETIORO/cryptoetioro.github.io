What is ETR?

ETIORO (ETR) is a completely decentralized token, designed to be simple in structure and functionality.

This means it is not controlled by any central entity and operates using blockchain technology, offering transparency and security to its users.

Bitcoin Cash (BCH) technology and its influence in ETIORO:

Bitcoin Cash (BCH) is a cryptocurrency that emerged as a fork of Bitcoin, intended to offer faster transactions and lower fees. It uses a similar technology to Bitcoin, based on a blockchain that supports fast and secure transactions. If ETIORO (ETR) is built or runs on the Bitcoin Cash platform, it can benefit from these features, such as shorter confirmation times and lower transaction costs, thanks to the BCH infrastructure.
